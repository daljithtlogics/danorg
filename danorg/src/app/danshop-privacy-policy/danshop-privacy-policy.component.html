Privacy Policy

DAN is committed to protecting your privacy

Your personal information is only used to process orders and provide you with the highest level of service

Information Collected

  • When you order a product or register on the DAN Shop, we need to know your name, email address, cell or landline number, billing and delivery address and your DAN number if you are a DAN member.  This enables us to process and fulfil your orders and notify you of your order status as well as allocate appropriate member discounts
  • We use the information collected to occasionally notify you about important functionality upgrades to the DAN Shop and other announcements we believe you will find valuable

Protecting your Information

  • To protect your personal information, we take reasonable precautions and follow industry best practices to make sure it is not inappropriately lost, misused, accessed, disclosed, altered or destroyed
  • We follow all PCI-DSS requirements and implement additional generally accepted industry standards
  • Your personal information is encrypted
  • The data exchanged between your browser and our web server is encrypted using transport layer security. Look for the lock icon next to the https://danshop.co.za/ URL
  • The DAN Shop does not store your banking or credit card details.
  • Payment at checkout is handled by either PayFast, SagePay, PayPal or you have the option to pay when collecting your order from the DAN Shop

Cookies

  • Identifies current customer session
  • Your preferred currency information
  • Your preferred language
  • Your browser will automatically accept cookies, however you may disable this if you wish to

Disclosing Information

The DAN Shop does not sell, trade or rent your personal information to others.
